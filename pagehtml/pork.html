Slow Cooker Pork and Sauerkraut

description:

Easy pork and sauerkraut slow-cooked with potatoes. Use a slow cooker liner for effortless cleanup. This dish is also delicious served with mashed potatoes if you don't want to use whole baby potatoes. I usually double the recipe as leftovers are even better the next day.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 8 hrs
  • Total Time: 8hrs 10 mins
  • Servings: 4

ingredients:

  • 1 (1 pound) whole pork tenderloin
  • 1 (24 ounce) bag baby potatoes, unpeeled (Optional)
  • 1 (20 ounce) can sauerkraut, undrained
  • 1 cup water, or more as needed
  • Â¼ cup butter, cubed
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

step preparation:

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Place whole pork tenderloin into a slow cooker. Arrange potatoes around pork; pour sauerkraut and juice over pork and potatoes. Add 1 cup water, butter cubes, salt, and black pepper.

  3. Cook on Low until pork is tender, 8 to 10 hours. Add more water after 8 hours if mixture seems dry.
